Parallel Coordinates Diagram

  • Each vertical axis represents one of the investigated parameters (e.g., constituent content or electrochemical activity).
  • Each line represents one MA's composition and activity across all axes.
  • You can interactively filter the dataset by clicking and dragging on the axis ranges. Multiple filters can be created. Filters can be removed by clicking on them
  • As a measure of activity the current densities are compared at spcific potentials (HER: -100 mV vs. RHE, OER 1700 mV vs. RHE, ORR: 800 mV vs. RHE, NOxRR: 50 mV vs. RHE).
  • For the ORR, HER and NOxRR the lower the current density is, the more active is the catalyst. For the OER, the higher the current density is, the more active is the catalyst.
